## How to recreate this cozy corner

Start with the chair first, then add fairy lights and pillow covers to create the main cozy feeling. After that, use wall decor and a small planter to make the corner feel complete. The floor lamp can be added later if you want a more polished reading setup.

For a budget-friendly start, focus on the items that change the vibe quickly: the lounge chair, warm lights, pillows, and wall decor. These pieces create most of the cozy look without needing a full room makeover.
